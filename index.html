NBA Dataset Visualizations

by Gurmukh, Moiz and Gary

The dataset we are using is comes from Kaggle: https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/wyattowalsh/basketball and we will be doing 3 visualizations one focusing on physical attributes, one focusing on games and one focusing on player stats! Please see below for our three primary hypothesis

Hypothesis #1

Performing better in NBA strength and eligibility tests or having better physical stats is not directly related to players being drafted higher

To prove this hypothesis, we will create a bubble graph with various different physical attributes such as height, wingspan, benchpress and compare that with their draft number

Hypothesis #2

The more Pts a shot is worth, the lower the success rate. Also that teams play better/win more when they are away rather than at home

To prove this hypothesis, we will show various games that are played by NBA teams throughtout many seasons and correleate this data together as well as show the success rates of different shots made

Hypothesis #3

More points correlate to less assists and rebounds

To prove this hypothesis, we will show stats of the top players using a scatter plot and slope chart
